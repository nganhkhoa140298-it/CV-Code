Our Mission

We are dedicated to making a positive impact through community events. Our mission is to promote health and wellness, support charitable causes, and foster a sense of community through engaging and impactful events.

Our History

Charity Run 2024 was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals committed to making a difference in their community. Over the years, our event has grown significantly, attracting thousands of participants and raising substantial funds for various charitable organizations.
