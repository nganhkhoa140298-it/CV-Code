Countdown to Charity Run 2024

Event Venue

The event will take place at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC) in Hochiminh City. Below is the information on how to reach the venue:

  • Address: 799 Nguyen Van Linh, Tan Phu Ward, District 7, HCMC
  • Parking: Available on-site with ample space for participants and visitors.
  • Transportation: Accessible by public transport, with bus routes 20 and 72 stopping nearby.
