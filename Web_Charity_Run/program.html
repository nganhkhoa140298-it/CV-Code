Workshops and Activities

In addition to the main running events, we offer a variety of workshops and activities throughout the day:

  • Yoga Sessions: Join our guided yoga sessions to relax and unwind.
  • Nutrition Talks: Learn about healthy eating habits and nutrition from experts.
  • Fitness Demonstrations: Participate in fitness demonstrations and try out new workouts.
  • Mental Health Workshops: Attend workshops focused on mental well-being and stress management.
  • Family Fun Activities: Enjoy games and activities suitable for the whole family.
