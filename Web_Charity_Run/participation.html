How to Participate

Whether you want to run, volunteer, or sponsor, there's a place for you! Here's how:

As a Runner

Register online or at designated spots.

As a Volunteer

Sign up to help out on the day of the event. Volunteers receive a free t-shirt and meal vouchers.

As a Sponsor

Contact us to discuss sponsorship opportunities and benefits. Various packages available.

Registration Information

To register as a participant, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the registration page.
  2. Fill out the required information, including your name, contact details, and event selection.
  3. Choose your payment method and complete the transaction.
  4. Receive a confirmation email with your registration details.

Ensure you read the event rules and guidelines before registering.

Volunteer Opportunities

We welcome volunteers to join us in making the Charity Run 2024 a success. Volunteers play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth running of the event and creating a positive experience for all participants.

Volunteer roles include:

  • Event setup and teardown
  • Registration and check-in assistance
  • Water station management
  • Route marshals
  • First aid support

All volunteers receive a free t-shirt, meal vouchers, and a certificate of appreciation. Sign up as a volunteer by visiting our volunteer registration page.

Sponsorship Opportunities

Your sponsorship can make a huge difference! We offer various sponsorship packages that provide numerous benefits:

  • Brand visibility and promotion through event materials and media coverage
  • Opportunities to showcase your products and services
  • Networking opportunities with other sponsors and participants
  • Recognition during the event and in post-event communications

Contact us at sponsor@charityrun2024.com to learn more about how you can support us.

Event Rules and Guidelines

Please ensure you follow these rules and guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable event for everyone:

  • All participants must register and wear their assigned bibs during the event.
  • Follow the designated routes and listen to the instructions of the marshals.
  • Maintain a respectful and positive attitude towards other participants and volunteers.
  • Dispose of any waste in the provided bins to keep the venue clean.
  • If you feel unwell or require assistance, approach the nearest first aid station or volunteer.
